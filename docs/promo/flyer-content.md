## General Printing & Distribution Guidelines

### File Preparation
- **Format:** PDF for print
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Color Mode:** CMYK for professional print, RGB for home/office printing
- **Bleed:** 0.125 inch if printing to edge

### Cost-Saving Tips
- Print 4-up (4 per page) for small flyers
- Use black and white for large quantities
- Consider standard paper sizes to avoid custom cutting
- Community centers often have free printing for local resources

### Distribution Locations

**High Priority:**
- Laundromats
- Food pantries
- Community health centers
- Public libraries
- Unemployment offices
- Social services offices

**Medium Priority:**
- Coffee shops (community boards)
- Religious organizations
- Community centers
- Senior centers
- College campuses (student services)
- Public housing offices

**Additional Ideas:**
- Include in food bank distribution bags
- Hand out at farmers markets (SNAP/EBT locations)
- Post at dollar stores
- Share with social workers and case managers
- Distribute at free clinics

### Bilingual Considerations
- Print English/Spanish versions for diverse communities
- Include "Se Habla EspaÃ±ol" notice
- Consider other languages based on local demographics
- Keep layout simple to accommodate text expansion

### Accessibility
- Use minimum 12-point font for body text
- High contrast colors (dark text on light background)
- Clear, sans-serif fonts (Arial, Helvetica, Verdana)
- Avoid green/red combinations (color blindness)
- Include website URL in addition to QR code

### Legal Disclaimer
Include on all flyers:
*Disclaimer: Dental Guide provides educational resources and information. This is not medical or legal advice. Always consult qualified dental professionals for treatment decisions.*

## QR Code Generation Instructions

### Creating Your QR Code
1. Go to: https://www QR-code-generator.com (or similar free service)
2. Enter URL: https://dental-guide-eta.vercel.app
3. Size: Minimum 300x300 pixels (larger is better for print)
4. Format: PNG or SVG
5. Download and insert into flyer design

### QR Code Best Practices
- **Size:** Minimum 1x1 inch (2.5x2.5 cm), preferably 2x2 inches
- **Contrast:** Dark code on light background (standard black/white works best)
- **Quiet Zone:** Maintain white space around QR code (at least 1/4 inch border)
- **Testing:** Always test scan before printing - use multiple phone types
- **Error Correction:** Use highest error correction level (H) for durability

### Advanced Option: Multiple QR Codes
For library/professional flyers, consider creating feature-specific QR codes:
- Main site: dental-guide-eta.vercel.app
- Script Builder: dental-guide-eta.vercel.app/script-builder
- School Finder: dental-guide-eta.vercel.app/school-finder
- Care Guides: dental-guide-eta.vercel.app/care-guides
